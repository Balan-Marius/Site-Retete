Omlette


Description

The perfect breackfast to start the perfect day.

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 2 strips of bacon

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs in a bowl
  2. Whisk until homogenous
  3. Add a pinch of salt
  4. Fry the bacon in a hot oiled pan
  5. After the bacon is done, turn down the head and add the eggs
  6. Using a spatula, pull the edges towwards the center of the pan when they begin to forn a crust
