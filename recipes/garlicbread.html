Garlic Bread


Description

Quick and delicious garlic bread recipe for you and your family to enjoy.

Ingredients

  • Bread of your choice
  • 200g Butters
  • 5 Cloves of garlic
  • 4 Tablespoons of parsley
  • 1/2 cups of parmesan
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions

  1. Put the butter into a bowl
  2. Mince and add the garlic to the bowl
  3. Add the parsLey
  4. Mix until smooth then spread the garlic butter onto the bread
  5. Cook on an oven or preheated air fryer for 5 minutes at 180 degrees Celsius
  6. When there are 2 minutes left, add the parmesan
